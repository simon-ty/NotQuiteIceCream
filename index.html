Our Story

Not Quite Ice Cream is a local business based in West Pennant Hills. It was started by Shirley Chan who is a mum of two beautiful girls. 

Like every mum, Shirley knows children want their party food to be fun and colourful.  Shirley made  Yuminies ™  , stands for 'Yummy Minis' , for her daughter's birthday party to add the 'wow' factor for her and her little friends.  

Rest assured that the every Yuminies ™ formulation has been taste tested by Shirley's own children. 



About Yuminies ™

Yuminies are clusters of  yummy chocolate topping on a mini cone, measuring to be around 9 cm long.  We have Fruit and Nut as well as Rocky Road toppings. More are coming. They are hand- made with all natural quality ingredients, with no artificial flavours and no artifical colours*.  Yuminies are filled with 100% love and wholesome ingredients without all the additives and numbers.  Scroll down to read more about our ingredients.

Yuminies can be stored in room temperature. No refrigeration is needed which make them ideal in outdoor parties or functions. As long as they are stored in cool dry places and away from direct sunlight in air tight containers, they will last for at least one month (that is if they are not eaten first!). Refrigeration is not recommended as we use Belgium converture chocolate. Temperature fluctuations may cause the chocolate to 'bloom' - a white colour layer on top of the chocolate.  Blooming does not affect the quality of chocolate, just the appearance. 


Yuminies are great for birthday parties, weddings, functions, baby showers and girls night-in. Equally good are they for unwinding at the end of a busy day.  

*except on certain chocolate drizzle

Our Ingredients

Shirley only uses ingredients that she feels comfortable for her own children. All ingredients are carefully selected so to include good nutrition without sacrificing the fun factor. Dried fruits, chopped nuts and cereals grains are incorporated in the fruit and nut Yuminies. Rocky Road Yuminies will contain marshmallow, coconut and gummie lollies. Chocolate is selected on the basis of cocoa mass and taste. Belgium converture chocolate is used for coating, not compound chocolate *. No filler ingredients are added, such as sugar, vegetable oil, glucose syrup, high fructose corn syrup or shortening. There are no artificial flavours or colours in any of the Yuminies*.   

Gluten free Yuminies are also available - they are equally delicious. Using her knowledge as a qualified dietitian, Shirely checks every single ingredient to ensure they are gluten free. In addition, Shirley also uses separate utensils and equipment to ensure no cross contamination can occur.
